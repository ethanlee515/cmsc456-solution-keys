\section*{Problem 3}

\subsection*{Part a}

We show that all ciphertexts are equally likely.
For all messages $m$ and ciphertext $c$, we have
\begin{align*}
    \Pr_{k}[c=\mathsf{Enc}(m)]&=\Pr_{P\in\Sym(2^n)}[P(m)=c]\\
    &=\frac{1}{2^n}
\end{align*}
since there are $2^n$ equally likely outcomes that $P(m)$ could map to.
